Transparency

The JFC has a limited amount of funds that it allocates to every student organization on campus. I will ensure organizations have a voice at every step of the process so that they are able to answer any questions the JFC has in relation to their budget.

I will contact organizations the JFC is allocating weekly so that the JFC can obtain any relevant information necessary to complete their allocation.

Organization Finances

Organization Finances

Work with each organization to assist them in responsible fiscal practices by assigning a representative to work with the organization throughout the year. This representative will be responsible for helping the organization maintain their internal budget and assist them with events, communicating with SLICE, and creating their budget for the next year.

By involving the representative, the JFC will be able to understand the organization and efficiently allocate its money so that organizations have the money they need to do the events they want to do.

JFC Structure and Method

JFC Structure and Method

Restructure the JFC to reduce meeting times which would allow more time for organizations to have input into the allocation their organization receives that year.

To reduce meeting times, I will increase JFC member's access to the budgets and allow them to get comfortable with the process before we begin the allocation process. Another method to reduce the time is by automating the input of metrics so that we can focus on the important things in your organizations budget.
